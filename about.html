The Requisite 3rd Person Spiel

Khalila Hughes is a designer, writer, and linguist with an obsession for trivia and making lists based out of Chicago, Illinois.

Once upon a time.

Hello web wanderer, welcome to my personal internet hub. So, where should I begin? I guess the beginning usually works.

I grew up in Los Angeles, California, daughter of an entrepreneur and educator. After going to school to study foreign languages, I took a brief dip into the fashion world to satisfy my urge to learn how to make clothing professionally. Then, for a change of scene, I headed east on what turned out to be a permanent detour to my father's hometown of Chicago.

Fast forward nearly eight years later and I’ve worked mostly in education building up a diverse skill set in, administration, project coordination and presentation design. I currently work as Project Manager and Associate Designer at Next Door Multimedia, a website and app development company.

I've always been interested in communicating with people or facilitating the communication between people, evidenced by my penchant for learning languages. For years I've searched for ways to combine my love of design and communicating and didn’t realize that my digital doodling could become a career. Now, that’s what I do.
